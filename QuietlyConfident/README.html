Quietly Confident User Manual

Participant and Administrator Functionality

Creating a playlist:

Press the "+" button immediately next to the Playlist List

This will open an alert which will prompt you for the name of the playlist you wish to create

Showing playlists and videos

Playlists are listed under the Playlists on the initial loading screen, and clicking it will release a drop down menu

All video segments that have been uploaded are shown in the local library, and the remote ones are found further below. All videos are presented in order as they are played in the playlist

Deleting playlists and videos

To delete any playlist or video simply click the trash can icon next to it and it will be deleted from the library

Appending videos to playlists

If you'd like to append a video to a playlist, whether the video is local or remote, you can do so by clicking "Add to Playlist..." right above the text of the video you wish to add

Removing video segments from playlists

In order to remove a video segment from a playlist, click Remove from Playlist below the video you wish to remove.

Searching for videos (local and remote)

Using the magnifying glass, above either local or remote video collections, type in (precisely) letters from the character's name or the transcript of the video you are looking for

Note: You can search via the magnifying glass in either view, the list will be a composite of the two libraries

Play playlist

To play a playlist, click on the videos you wish to play as listed from left to right on the webpage and enjoy.

Removing video segments from playlists

In order to remove a video segment from a playlist, click Remove from Playlist below the video you wish to remove.

Administrator-Only Functionality

Mark and unmark local video segments

Using the drop-down menu select whether you want each video to be public or private, the default is public

Registering third parties

To register a third party site, click the + button above the remote libraries and paste the url of the remote library you'd like to insert.
